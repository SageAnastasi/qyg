1 Quantify Your Gays: The Statistics Of Queer Death In TV

Sage Anastasi, August 2018

1.0.1 About the author & contact information

I have a degree in statistics and communication studies, and am currently a PhD student in data science. I can be contacted via my blog. The data and analysis scripts for this project are in this site's github repo.

In 2020 I expanded this work into a dissertation for Honours Communication Studies, titled "Her Robot Girlfriend: Queer Death and Resurrection in Science Fiction TV", focussing on the specific cases of The 100, Person Of Interest, and Black Mirror's episode "San Junipero". A PDF of this work is hosted in this site's github repo.
